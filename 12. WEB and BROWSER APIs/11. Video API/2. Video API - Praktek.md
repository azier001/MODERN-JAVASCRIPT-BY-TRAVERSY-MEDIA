<a name="ringkasan-method-dan-event"></a>
## ğŸ“š Ringkasan Method dan Event

### **Method Video API**

| Method | Deskripsi | Contoh |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| `play()` | Memutar video | `video.play()` |
| `pause()` | Menjeda video | `video.pause()` |
| `load()` | Memuat ulang video | `video.load()` |

### **Properti Video API**

| Properti | Deskripsi | Contoh |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| `currentTime` | Waktu saat ini (detik) | `video.currentTime = 10` |
| `duration` | Durasi total video | `video.duration` |
| `volume` | Volume (0.0 - 1.0) | `video.volume = 0.5` |
| `muted` | Status mute | `video.muted = true` |

### **Event Video API**

| Event | Deskripsi | Kapan Dipicu |
|-------|-----------|--------------|
| `play` | Video mulai diputar | Saat `.play()` dipanggil |
| `pause` | Video dijeda | Saat `.pause()` dipanggil |
| `timeupdate` | Waktu berubah | Setiap detik saat video berjalan |
| `ended` | Video selesai | Saat video mencapai akhir |

### **Atribut HTML Video**

| Atribut | Deskripsi | Contoh |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| `src` | Sumber file video | `src="video.mp4"` |
| `width` | Lebar video | `width="500"` |
| `height` | Tinggi video | `height="300"` |
| `controls` | Tampilkan kontrol | `controls` |
| `autoplay` | Putar otomatis | `autoplay` |
| `muted` | Tanpa suara | `muted` |
| `loop` | Putar berulang | `loop` |
| `poster` | Gambar thumbnail | `poster="img.png"` |
